StoryDeck
Make long-form content feel alive on mobile.

Most content teams already have words, examples, and key points. The hard part is turning that material into something people actually want to read on a phone.

StoryDeck starts from rough notes and shapes them into a page with rhythm, emphasis, and structure.

- Works for launches, explainers, internal storytelling, and lightweight campaign pages
- Gives teams a reusable format instead of asking AI to generate a fresh HTML page every time
- Exports a standalone file that can be shared, archived, or hosted quickly

The first version is intentionally narrow. It focuses on speed, polish, and repeatability instead of trying to become a full website builder.

If the base workflow works, the next wave is easy to imagine: richer modules, stronger themes, and scenario-specific content packs.
