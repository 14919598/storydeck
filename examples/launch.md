## Why this matters
Teams usually do not have a content problem. They have a packaging problem. The message exists, but it still needs to become a page that feels readable, deliberate, and ready to share on mobile.

## What makes StoryDeck different
- Repeatable structure: The page starts from a story system instead of a blank HTML file.
- Visual range: The same content can feel editorial, corporate, or launch-ready.
- Portable output: One export produces a standalone HTML page that can travel anywhere.

## Launch flow
- Draft | Paste notes, Markdown, or JSON into the workspace.
- Shape | Pick a skeleton and a theme that match the message.
- Share | Export the final HTML file and publish it anywhere.

## Proof points
- Preview speed: The mobile frame updates instantly.
- Reuse value: The same engine supports launches, explainers, and internal stories.
- Lower friction: You focus on the content and story rhythm instead of layout code.

## Quote
> The goal is not one impressive page. The goal is a workflow that can keep producing them.
